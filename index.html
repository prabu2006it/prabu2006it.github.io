About Our Foundation

Wings and Wonders Foundation is a beautiful natural sanctuary offering a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Spanning over 50 acres of lush greenery, our park provides a refreshing environment for all visitors.

Our park features beautiful walking trails, picnic areas, playgrounds for children, and serene spots for relaxation and meditation.
